Salt Of The Earth clone process

 

Step 1

It all starts with a healthy thriving mom plant. We then select the healthiest nodes from the mom and use proper clean nutrients for optimal growth.

 

Step 2

We ensure optimal cloning by adhering to proper sterile techniques so there is small to no chance of cross contamination when rooting and transplanting.

 

Step 3

Finally, with proper humidity and water intake- by week 3 the plants have healthy root growth and are ready for transplant.
